Help

  1. How do I create an account?

    To create an account, click on the 'Sign Up' button on the login page, fill in your details, and submit the form. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

  2. How do I reset my password?

    If you've forgotten your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. Enter your email address, and you'll receive a link to reset your password.

  3. How can I update my profile information?

    To update your profile information, log in to your account, go to the 'Profile' section, make the necessary changes, and save your updates.

  4. How do I use the vulnerability checker tool?

    To use the vulnerability checker tool, navigate to the 'Vulnerability Checker' section, paste your JavaScript code into the provided text area, and click 'Check for Vulnerabilities.' The tool will analyze your code and provide a report of any detected vulnerabilities.

  5. What should I do if I find a vulnerability?

    If you find a vulnerability, it is crucial to address it immediately. Follow the recommendations provided in the vulnerability report to fix the issue. If you need further assistance, contact our support team.

  6. How can I contact support?

    You can contact support by clicking on the 'Contact Support' link in the footer of the website. Fill out the support form with your details and issue, and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.

  7. How do I enable multi-factor authentication?

    To enable multi-factor authentication, go to the 'Security Settings' section of your profile, select 'Enable Multi-Factor Authentication,' and follow the on-screen instructions to set it up.

  8. What is the best way to secure my application?

    Securing your application involves multiple steps, including regularly updating your software, using secure coding practices, implementing multi-factor authentication, and continuously monitoring for vulnerabilities.

  9. How do I report a security issue?

    To report a security issue, go to the 'Report a Security Issue' section on our website, fill out the form with details about the issue, and submit it. Our security team will review your report and take appropriate action.

  10. How can I learn more about cybersecurity?

    To learn more about cybersecurity, you can explore our 'Resources' section, which includes articles, tutorials, and guides on various cybersecurity topics. Additionally, consider enrolling in cybersecurity courses and certifications.
